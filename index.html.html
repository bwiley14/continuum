Our Story

Built from experience, designed with empathy

Continuum was born from watching my family coordinate care for my grandfather.

I watched my family struggle not because they didn't care enough—but because we had no good way to stay coordinated. That's when I realized: families facing memory conditions don't need another complicated medical app. They need something simple, compassionate, and designed for the reality of caregiving together.

Continuum is what I wish we'd had. A single place where everyone who loves someone can stay connected, informed, and working as a team. Because the people caring for our loved ones deserve tools that make life easier, not harder.
